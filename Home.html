IEEE INDICON

INDICON is the flagship annual international conference of the IEEE India Council. INDICON has been the most prestigious conference conceptualized by IEEE India Council in the field of Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering & Electronics and Communication Engineering, in general.

The first INDICON conference has been hosted by IEEE Kharagpur Section in 2004. After 20 years IEEE Kharagpur Section is hosting INDICON 2024.

  • Computer Architecture and Embedded Systems
  • Next-Gen Communications, Networks, and IoT
